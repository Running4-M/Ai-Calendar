What This AI Calendar Does

This tool is designed to help you plan and prepare for tasks ahead of time, not just jot down quick notes or reminders. It’s all about getting a head start on tasks that require creativity or preparation. Here’s how it works:

  • Plan Ahead: Spend 10 minutes at the start of the week to create events for all the tasks you plan to work on. This lets the AI generate tailored responses on the scheduled day, saving you time and effort.
  • AI-Driven Responses: On the day of the event, the AI will deliver customized responses to kickstart your tasks, such as brainstorm ideas, outlines, or strategies for roadblocks.
  • Interactive Refinement: Use the "Start Chat" feature to adjust the AI’s responses to your specific needs for a personalized experience.
  • Flexible Scheduling: Drag and drop events to adjust timing as needed. AI responses are generated only on the scheduled day to provide relevant support.
